📊 Project Title

Adventure-Works-Sales-Dashboard


🧩 **Business Problem

Businesses need more than just sales reports—they require a holistic view of both revenue performance and customer behavior to drive growth.

Key challenges include:

Identifying high-value vs low-value customers
Understanding customer purchase patterns and loyalty
Tracking sales performance across regions and products
Reducing revenue loss from returns and low-performing products

Without proper segmentation and analytics, companies struggle to target the right customers and optimize strategy.

💼 Solution

Developed a comprehensive Power BI dashboard solution integrating:

Sales Performance Analysis
Territory & Product Insights
Customer Segmentation using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)

This solution enables stakeholders to move from descriptive analytics → actionable business insights.
1️⃣Executive Summary

Provides a high-level overview of overall business performance.

Key Features:
KPIs: Total Revenue, Profit, Profit Margin, Orders, Returns
Monthly sales and order trends
Year-over-year performance comparison
Purpose:

Enables stakeholders to quickly assess overall business health and track growth trends.

🌍Territory Analysis

Focuses on geographical sales performance across regions.

Key Features:
Sales and profit distribution by region
Map visualization for geographic insights
Regional comparison of orders, revenue, and profitability
Purpose:

Helps identify high-performing regions and supports location-based strategic decisions.

📦Product Analysis

Analyzes product-level performance and profitability.

Key Features:
Category → Subcategory → Product drill-down
Top and bottom performing products
Profit margin and return analysis
Product-wise sales contribution
Purpose:

Identifies high-performing products and highlights areas with low profitability or high returns.
👥 Customer Analysis (RFM Segmentation)
Provides insights into customer behavior and value.

Key Features:
RFM Segmentation (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)
Customer grouping (e.g., Champions, Loyal, At Risk)
Monthly trend of New vs Returning Customers
Key metrics:
Returning Customers
Average Order Value (AOV)
Customer-level revenue

Implemented RFM segmentation to classify customers based on:

Recency – How recently a customer made a purchase
Frequency – How often they purchase
Monetary Value – How much they spend
🎯 Business Value of RFM:
Identifies high-value loyal customers for retention strategies
Detects at-risk customers who haven’t purchased recently
Helps design targeted marketing campaigns
Supports personalized business decisions
Purpose:

Supports customer retention strategies, targeted marketing, and identification of high-value customers.

📌 Conclusion

The analysis reveals that overall sales performance has shown a steady upward trend, with certain periods experiencing accelerated growth driven by increased order volume. However, profitability is not uniform across all products, as some high-revenue items generate lower margins and exhibit higher return rates, indicating opportunities for cost optimization and quality improvement.

From a customer perspective, RFM segmentation highlights that a significant portion of revenue is driven by high-value segments such as Champions and Loyal customers. At the same time, the presence of “At Risk” and “About to Sleep” segments suggests potential churn, emphasizing the need for targeted retention strategies.

The monthly trend of new vs. returning customers shows a growing base of returning customers, indicating improving customer retention. Additionally, the analysis of Average Order Value (AOV) provides insights into customer spending behavior, which can be leveraged for pricing and promotional strategies.

Overall, the dashboard uncovers key opportunities to:

Strengthen customer retention and engagement
Optimize product performance and reduce returns
Focus on high-performing regions and customer segments

These insights support more informed, data-driven decision-making across sales, marketing, and product strategy.
